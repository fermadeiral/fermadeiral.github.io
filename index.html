About

I'm a Brazilian researcher, and I recently defended a Ph.D. in Computer Science at Federal University of Uberlândia (2019), advised by Marcelo Maia (see LASCAM). I spent six months in a "sandwich doctorate" internship at Inria Lille (France), supervised by Martin Monperrus. I obtained my master's degree from the São Paulo State University (2013) and my bachelor's degree from the University of Western São Paulo (2010), both in Computer Science. My research interests are on Software Engineering, especially on software maintenance and evolution, and mining software repositories.

PS: my complete name is Fernanda Madeiral Delfim. Currently, I use only my first last name, "Madeiral", although you can eventually find some of my works with the last last name, "Delfim" :)
